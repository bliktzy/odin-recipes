Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

Spaghetti Bolognese is a classic Italian dish made with ground beef, tomatoes, onions, garlic, and spices, served over spaghetti pasta. It's a hearty, comforting meal that's perfect for a family dinner or a special occasion. The ground beef is simmered in a rich tomato sauce, making it a flavorful and satisfying dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti pasta
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti pasta according to package instructions, until al dente.
  2. Meanwhile, in a large pan over medium-high heat, brown the ground beef until fully cooked. Drain off any excess fat.
  3. Add the onion and garlic to the pan and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Bring the mixture to a simmer and let cook for 10-15 minutes, until the sauce has thickened.
  5. Drain the cooked spaghetti and add it to the pan with the sauce. Toss until the spaghetti is evenly coated in the sauce.
  6. Serve hot, topped with grated Parmesan cheese, if desired.
