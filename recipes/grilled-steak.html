Grilled Steak

Description

Grilled steak is a simple and delicious way to prepare steak. Seasoned with salt, pepper, and garlic, this steak is grilled to perfection, resulting in a juicy and flavorful meal. It's a versatile dish that can be served with a variety of sides, from roasted vegetables to mashed potatoes. Perfect for a special occasion or a quick weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 (1-inch-thick) bone-in or boneless steak
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic

Steps

  1. Remove the steak from the refrigerator and let it sit at room temperature for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Preheat grill to high heat.
  3. Season the steak with salt, pepper, and minced garlic.
  4. Grill the steak for 4 to 6 minutes per side for medium-rare, or to your desired level of doneness.
  5. Remove the steak from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
