Greek Yogurt and Honey Parfait

Description

This recipe is a simple and healthy breakfast option that is perfect for those who are looking for a quick and easy breakfast. A Greek yogurt parfait is made by layering Greek yogurt and honey in a glass. The yogurt is rich in protein and the honey is a natural sweetener. To make it more delicious, you can add some fresh fruits such as blueberries, strawberries or granola to the parfait. It is a perfect way to start your day with a nutritious and delicious meal.
